Introduction

An Introduction into Session 2 and what you will complete by the end of the Session. How the Scrum roles will be utilised throughout the Session
Scrum Meeting! Sprint Planning

Based on the Product Backlog You created in Session 1, you will be asked to analyse, prioritise and define a list of requirements to be implemented during the first Sprint. You will be following Scrum methods in order to gather these requirements. You will then submit these requirements in order to continue.

Scrum Meeting! Daily Scrum

You will be conducting a daily scrum meeting. You will base the content of this meeting on the tasks you have been assigned to complete on this sprint. You will then submit what you discussed during the meeting.

Scrum Meeting! Sprint Review & Retrospective

In this task you will complete an informal review of the progress made during the sprint. You will then document an assessment of the quality of the sprint based on the requirements, assessing whether the assigned tasks were detailed enough, if there are any new requirements that need to be made and any other issues faces during the sprint. You will then submit a document in order to conclude the first Sprint.
